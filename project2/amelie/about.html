Amelie Fawson is a multimedia journalist passionate about crafting stories that resonate across senses. As the Audio-Visual Assistant Editor at The Daily Tar Heel, she oversees a team of 18, shaping the creative direction of weekly video series and special projects. With experience as an audio reporter and podcast contributor, Amelie thrives at the intersection of audio, video and writing to deliver impactful narratives. During her time at The Watauga Democrat, she wrote over 65 articles and captured photographs that illuminated local communities. With a strong foundation in AP Style, Adobe Premiere Pro and HTML/CSS, Amelie brings technical expertise and a deep commitment to storytelling.
